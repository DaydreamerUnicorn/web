## Digital Growth Manager

<About />

The Digital Growth Marketing Manager is responsible for developing and managing the digital demand strategy and 
Ory marketing campaigns across paid search, paid social and sponsored publisher channels. 
This role will drive digital campaign execution across integrated marketing objectives, and will develop and manage demand execution to drive 
product adoption and meet pipeline goals from both inbound and outbound campaigns. 

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: HubSpot, Google Analytics

### What You'll Do

- Build customer focused programs and digital campaigns to help drive data-driven strategic campaign recommendations.
- Create and expand digital channels to engage prospects and customers to quickly scale acquisition strategy and frive retention.
- Deliver insights to drive campaign optimization and identify personalized conversion journeys across different groups of users.
- Analyze and report data across all existing channels to monitor marketing site growth.
- Manage paid demand generation strategy including budgets (project plans) to assist in building sales pipeline by driving high quality leads.
- Build and adjust marketing operations infrastructure that incorporates performance measurements and analytics.
- Collaborate with team members (and perhaps an external agency) to execute on SEO priorities to increase organic traffic to Ory Corp and associated websites.
- Collaborate with other departments (HR, Sales, Engineering) to maximize yield from shared channels and programs.
- Drive innovation and experimentation to improve demand creation & conversion.
- Test new lead generation approaches and share key insights.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Experience with modern digital growth strategies and technical/industry experience focused on SEO, SEM, paid social and/or web conversion rate optimization to improve lead generation, sales pipeline, and revenue.
- Understanding performance data on a deep level (CAC, LTV, Conversion rate, ROAS (return on ad spend) Customer retention rates and churn).
- Experience in successfully building and improving paid demand generating campaigns for product-focused SaaS enterprises in the B2B market.
- Strong analytical skills with an ability to compile and analyze campaign data to inform strategic decisions at any part of the funnel.
- Experience working closely with technical product marketing managers to amplify key content and features.
- Experience leading cross-department projects and collaborating with web developers to drive meaningful impact.
- Entrepreneurial mindset, hands-on attitude and very important, detail orientated
- Fun collaboration across departments
- Strong editorial skills in English
