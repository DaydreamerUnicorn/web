## Senior Performance Marketing manager

<About />

As a senior performance marketing manager at Ory, you will be responsible for
generating new customers and leads for the Ory Cloud through paid media
channels. You will be responsible for strategy, execution excellence and
accountability reporting. You will play a key role in the development of
performance marketing and decide on key success factors. With product management
and your marketing colleagues you will regularly assess the product and the
market.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location:** Munich, Germany
- **Components:** Google Analytics, CMS Plattforms

### What you'll do

- Ownership over the performance marketing of Ory.
- Creation of the performance marketing strategy and goals. This includes
  audience targeting, action plan, budgeting and performance analytics.
- Design and implementation of all performance marketing efforts and campaigns.
- Evaluating and deciding on software and tools for use in performance
  marketing.
- Setting up and optimizing relevant paid media channels such as Google Ads,
  LinkedIn Ads and other new advertising platforms relevant to the target group.
- Creative design of ads and landing pages as well as related A/B testing and
  performance analysis.
- Creating performance dashboards, analytics requirements and market
  intelligence reports.
- Conducting regular marketing insights sessions for your Ory colleagues.
- Participate in strategy sessions with Ory management.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Fluent in written and spoken English.
- A strong passion for the internet and data privacy, security and transparency.
- A strong understanding of customer needs and a drive to provide them with
  sustainable solutions.
- Three to five years of professional experience in digital marketing.
- An excellent understanding of conversion funnels and customer journeys.
- Hands-on experience with marketing software, automations and tools.
- A strong affinity for data and strong analytical skills.
- Strong interest in marketing trends and best practices.
- An understanding of web technologies such as the HTTP protocol, HTML and
  Javascript.
- Ideally marketing experience in SaaS, B2B or software environments.
