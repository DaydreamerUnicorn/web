## Technical SEO Analyst

<About />

As Technical SEO Analyst, you will implement SEO best practices to optimize
Ory's reach as a brand and global standard for secure identity management
solutions.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: DeepCrawl, Screaming Frog, and SEO Rada

### What You'll Do

- Lead technical SEO efforts in collaboration with web development and product
  teams
- Assist on general SEO projects such as keyword research, content planning, and
  other internal SEO lead work
- Monitor Ory‘s web presence – finding and resolving technical or content issues
  that arise
- Help us determine internal web content accessibility standards and understand
  how they impact SEO
- Communicate SEO best practices to the rest of the marketing team, and make
  sure they are being applied
- Devise a best practice documentation scheme to communicate with engineering
  teams
- Work with growth marketing experts to foresee impact to SEO during A/B testing
  experiments
- Communicate SEO roadmap to relevant members of company leadership
- Maintain your knowledge of industry best practices and innovations in
  technical SEO
- Assist on wider SEO objectives such as site migrations, page deprecations, and
  more

### We’re looking for someone who has

- 4 + years of experience working in technical SEO with multiple CMS platforms
- Understanding of core SEO principles
- Ability to collaborate across departments, including engineering
- A technical understanding of HTML and JavaScript frameworks and how to
  optimize them (additional frameworks and other programming languages are a
  bonus)
- Experience working with different crawlers to identify technical issues (i.e.
  DeepCrawl, Screaming Frog, and SEO Radar)
- Ability to communicate technical concepts to a wide audience with varying
  levels of technical expertise
- Knowledge of Core Web Vitals and their impact on SEO
- Ability to develop and understand page schema
- Understanding of sitemaps and hreflang
- Understanding of how SEO works in relation to Google crawls
