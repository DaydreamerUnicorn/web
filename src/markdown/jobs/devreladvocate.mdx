## Development Relations Advocate

<About />

Ory is the largest open-source authentication and authorization (login,
registration, MFA, permissions, OAuth2) ecosystem and community in the world. We
develop open-source software and use it to build a scalable, easy-to-use, and
secure cloud platform. You will provide organizational support to Ory leadership
to ensure smooth operations and communications. Together with the CEO, you will
provide a core leadership function that collaborates with UX design,
engineering, finance, marketing, and data analytics.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: Ory Open Source, Github, Slack, Socials

### What You'll Do

- Collaborate with product managers, engineering, and digital marketing to understand the suite of Ory products
Create top-notch content to engage and inspire developers of all skill levels
- Champion Ory’s developer products in-person and online via conferences, industry events and writing technical tutorials, articles and videos 
- Be the “first customer” for our products and help developers discover, implement, and succeed with our APIs and SDKs, continuously improving the developer experience
- Identify and cultivate strategic partnerships to grow our developer community
- Influence developer products after gathering insights from our developer community


### We’re looking for someone who has

- Creative drive, empathy, and excellent communication skills
- Facility in communicating with diverse audiences, large and small
- Facility in communicating technical/abstract concepts with simplicity and elegance
- Hands-on full-stack development experience
- Intellectual curiosity and sharp problem solving skills
- Bonus: experience at a SaaS company or startup

### Please include these in your application:

- Projects & code you’re proud of
- Products you’ve helped develop
- Links to your dev-related online presence (i.e. blog or profile in online communities like GitHub, Twitter, Quora, or Stack Overflow)
- Languages you are fluent in, both human and technical
- What you find most interesting about Ory
