## Brand & Content Marketing Manager

<About />

As a Senior Technical SEO Marketing Manager at Ory, you will be responsible for
generating new customers and leads for the Ory Cloud via search engines. You
will be responsible for strategy, execution excellence and accountability
reporting. You will play a key role in the development of SEO and decide on key
success factors. Together with the Product Management and your Marketing
colleagues you will regularly assess the product and the market.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location:** Munich, Germany

### What you'll do

- Ownership of the SEO of Ory.
- Creation of the SEO strategy and goals. This includes keyword targeting,
  action plan, budgeting and performance analytics.
- Design and implementation of all SEO measures.
- Evaluation of and decision on software and tools for use in SEO.
- Building and optimizing relevant SEO rankings.
- SEO design of landing pages and CMS templates and associated A/B testing and
  performance analytics.
- Creating performance dashboards, analytics requirements and market
  intelligence reports.
- Conducting regular marketing insights sessions for your Ory colleagues.
- Participate in strategy sessions with Ory management.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Fluent in written and spoken English.
- A strong passion for the internet and data privacy, security and transparency.
- A strong understanding of customer needs and a drive to provide them with
  sustainable solutions.
- Three to five years of professional experience in digital marketing.
- An excellent understanding of conversion funnels and customer journeys.
- Hands-on experience with SEO software, CMS systems and core web vitals.
- A strong affinity for data and strong analytical skills.
- Strong interest in marketing trends and best practices.
- A very good understanding of web technologies such as the HTTP protocol, HTML
  and Javascript.
- Ideally marketing experience in SaaS, B2B or software environments.
- Familiarity with productivity tools such as spreadsheets, Python or web APIs
  is an advantage.
